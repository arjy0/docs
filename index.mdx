## What is Sonarly?

Sonarly is an AI-powered code review tool that helps development teams catch bugs, improve code quality, and ship faster. It integrates seamlessly into your workflow and provides instant, actionable feedback on every pull request.

## Who it's for

Sonarly is built for development teams who want to maintain high code quality without slowing down their velocity. Whether you're a startup or an enterprise, Sonarly scales with your needs.
