## Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does setup take?">
    Most teams complete onboarding in under 5 minutes. You'll connect your stack, install our monitoring agent, and run your first test.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I need to write tests?">
    No! Sonarly automatically generates and runs tests for your application. You just need to connect your stack.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when an issue is detected?">
    You'll receive an alert through your configured notification channel (email, Slack, etc.) with details about what broke and when.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My monitoring agent won't connect">
    Check that:
    - Your API key is correct
    - Your firewall allows outbound connections to Sonarly
    - The agent has proper permissions in your environment
    
    Still stuck? Contact us at support@sonarly.com
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I'm not receiving alerts">
    Verify your notification settings in your dashboard. Make sure:
    - Your email/Slack is properly configured
    - Alerts aren't being filtered by your email provider
    - You've enabled notifications for the right severity levels
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I customize what gets monitored?">
    Yes! After onboarding, you can configure which endpoints, functions, or components to monitor in your dashboard settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
