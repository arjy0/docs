## What happens during onboarding

Our interactive onboarding will walk you through:

1. **Connect your stack** - Choose from HTML, NPM, Python, or Node
2. **Install monitoring** - Add our lightweight monitoring agent
3. **Configure alerts** - Set up how you want to be notified
4. **Run your first test** - We'll verify everything is working

That's it! You'll be monitoring your application in under 5 minutes.
